# ✦ LIA Weekly Reflection Log
*Week 5

> **The Purpose**
> Complete this once per week during your LIA. In an intense and fast-moving period, this reflection log helps you pause and notice how you are actually leading, not just what you are doing. Clarity and honesty matter far more than length.

---

### Ⅰ . Progress & Successes

**What Went Well?**
* What progress was made and what tangible outcomes were achieved?
* What am I proud of this week, and what specific factors contributed to this success?
* *How does this success link back to my [[SMART Goals]]?*

> [!quote] Reflection
> 在这里直接开始打字……
> 支持直接换行，体验非常丝滑。

### Ⅱ . Areas for Growth

**What Could Have Been Done Differently?**
* What did not go to plan, stalled, felt unclear, or required unexpected rework?
* Where did I misjudge timing, priorities, or interpersonal communication?
* *Focusing on learning rather than self-criticism, what would I approach differently next time?*

### Ⅳ . Ethical Engagement

**Reflecting through the lens of [[Ethical Engagement]]:**
* What interactions, decisions, or key moments stood out in how this work unfolded?
* How was my role positioned in relation to others (e.g., leading, supporting, learning, facilitating)?
* What impact did my contribution have—both intended and unintended?
* Did anything shift my understanding of the context, relationships, or longer-term implications of this work?
