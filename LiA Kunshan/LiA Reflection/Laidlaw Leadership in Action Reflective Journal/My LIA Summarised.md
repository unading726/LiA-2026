# ✦ My LIA Summarised
*Clarity of Purpose*

> Before your project begins, take time to articulate your Leadership in Action clearly and honestly. This page is about establishing a definitive clarity of purpose.

---

### Ⅰ . My LIA in 3-5 Sentences
* What is the core challenge you are engaging with?
* Who are you collaborating with on the ground?
* What concrete, tangible activities will fill your time during these six weeks?
*(Write this as if explaining your project to someone entirely outside the Laidlaw Programme.)*

> [!quote] Reflection
> 

---

### Ⅱ . The Bigger System
* What larger, macro-level system or structural issue is this project connected to (e.g., healthcare access, climate policy, education systems)?
* Is your short-term project addressing a root cause, a contributing factor, or a visible symptom?
* What would realistically need to shift at a systems level for this issue to change meaningfully?

> [!quote] Reflection
> 

---

### Ⅲ . Your Leadership Challenge
* What specific outcomes are you personally accountable for?
* Where will you need to actively take initiative rather than waiting for external direction?
* How do you plan to manage your own time, boundaries, and priorities?

> [!quote] Reflection
> 

---

### Ⅳ . Going Beyond Your Comfort Zone
* What elements of this specific context feel unfamiliar or challenging?
* Which of your existing skills will be tested most severely?
* What would "playing it safe" look like in this project, and how will you intentionally avoid doing so?

> [!quote] Reflection
> 

---

### Ⅴ . One Sentence Summary
*Distil your entire LIA framework into a single, grounded sentence.*

> [!quote] Project Statement
> During my LIA, I will [Action/Output], in collaboration with [Partner/Community], in order to [Intended Impact].
